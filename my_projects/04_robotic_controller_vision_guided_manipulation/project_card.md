**Vision-Guided Robotic Controllers** focus on the development of real-time control systems for industrial robots, integrating forward and inverse kinematics with EtherCAT-based multi-axis motion control. Vision and AI-based object detection are used to identify object type and position, enabling precise, autonomous manipulation and closed-loop robot–vision interaction.
