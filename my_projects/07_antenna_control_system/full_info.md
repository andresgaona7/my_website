## Antenna Maintenance & Control System (2023–2024)

### Challenge
Ensure **continuous satellite communication** with **minimal tracking error**, high system reliability, and built-in redundancy for long-term operation.

### Core Technologies
- Satellite tracking algorithms  
- Multi-axis motion control  
- Real-time feedback control  
- Sensor integration (position, feedback sensors)  
- Redundancy and fallback mechanisms  

### Contribution
- Designed and implemented an **automated antenna tracking system** achieving **≤ 2° tracking error**  
- Integrated multiple sensors with real-time feedback for accurate and stable tracking  
- Implemented **fallback and redundancy mechanisms** to improve system reliability  
- Utilized a **satellite tracking API** to enhance communication efficiency and robustness  

### Results & Impact
- High-precision satellite tracking with stable long-term operation  
- Improved communication reliability through redundancy-aware control  
- Deployed as part of an industrial antenna maintenance system
