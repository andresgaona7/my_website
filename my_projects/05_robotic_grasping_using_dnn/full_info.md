## Overview

This project focuses on **robotic grasping estimation** using a combination of **deep learning** and **evolutionary optimization** techniques.  
The system enables a robotic manipulator to reliably grasp **different objects with varying shapes and orientations**, achieving fast inference while maintaining high accuracy.

An evolutionary strategy is used to refine grasp candidates generated by a deep neural network, improving robustness and repeatability in real-world scenarios.

---

## Goal

Implement an **AI-based robotic grasping algorithm** capable of estimating reliable grasp configurations for different objects in cluttered environments.
